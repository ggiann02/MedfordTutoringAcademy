Our Services

Medford Tutoring Academy has always been an excellence in providing professional, targeted tutoring help to high-school students who have a prospect of attending college. We provide tutoring services for the two most important standardized tests for college admissions: SAT and ACT. Although our main tutoring center is based in Medford, Massachussetts, our tutoring services are offered both in-person and online to give equal opportunity to all students across the globe.
